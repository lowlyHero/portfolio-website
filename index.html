About Me

I love taking things apart and putting them back together again in order to figure out what makes things work! This passion for understanding how the world works "underneath the hood" eventually led me to explore how and why our world was being rapidly dominated by these little boxes located in homes across the world. This spark of curiosity turned into an obsession with computer programming. I quickly realized that to be able to communicate with a computer was to wield a tremendous power. My keyboard suddenly became my wand, and my computer became my book of spells.
